## Overview

Welcome to **Dual Mind** - the Browser-Based Multiplayer Game Suite! This platform features 10 classic games designed to provide entertainment while tracking your cognitive performance.

---

## Games

### 1. Tic Tac Toe
**Players:** 2 | **Difficulty:** Easy | **Time:** 1-3 minutes

**How to Play:**
- Players take turns placing X or O on a 3×3 grid
- First player to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins
- If all 9 squares are filled with no winner, it's a draw

**Skills Measured:** Pattern recognition, strategic planning

---

### 2. Four in a Row (Connect Four)
**Players:** 2 | **Difficulty:** Medium | **Time:** 3-10 minutes

**How to Play:**
- Players take turns dropping colored discs into a 7-column, 6-row grid
- Discs fall to the lowest available position in the column
- First player to connect 4 discs in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins

**Skills Measured:** Spatial reasoning, forward planning, threat detection

---

### 3. Checkers (Draughts)
**Players:** 2 | **Difficulty:** Medium | **Time:** 10-30 minutes

**How to Play:**
- Players move diagonally on dark squares
- Capture opponent pieces by jumping over them
- Reach the opposite end to become a "King" (can move backwards)
- Win by capturing all opponent pieces or blocking their moves

**Skills Measured:** Tactical thinking, multi-step planning

---

### 4. Dots and Boxes
**Players:** 2 | **Difficulty:** Easy | **Time:** 5-15 minutes

**How to Play:**
- Players take turns drawing lines between adjacent dots on a grid
- Complete a box (4 sides) to claim it and take another turn
- Player with the most boxes when the grid is complete wins

**Skills Measured:** Pattern recognition, strategic timing

---

### 5. Memory Match
**Players:** 1-2 | **Difficulty:** Easy | **Time:** 2-5 minutes

**How to Play:**
- Cards are laid face down in a grid
- Flip two cards per turn to find matching pairs
- If cards match, keep them and take another turn
- Player with the most pairs wins

**Skills Measured:** Short-term memory, concentration, pattern recall

---

### 6. Word Chain
**Players:** 2+ | **Difficulty:** Medium | **Time:** 5-15 minutes

**How to Play:**
- First player says a word
- Next player must say a word starting with the last letter of the previous word
- Words cannot be repeated
- Player who cannot think of a valid word loses

**Skills Measured:** Vocabulary, quick thinking, language processing

---

### 7. Game 24
**Players:** 1+ | **Difficulty:** Hard | **Time:** 1-5 minutes per puzzle

**How to Play:**
- Four numbers are given (1-13)
- Use +, -, ×, ÷ and parentheses to make exactly 24
- Each number must be used exactly once
- First to find a solution wins the round

**Skills Measured:** Arithmetic fluency, logical reasoning, problem-solving

---

### 8. Mini Sudoku (4×4)
**Players:** 1 | **Difficulty:** Easy-Medium | **Time:** 2-10 minutes

**How to Play:**
- Fill in a 4×4 grid with numbers 1-4
- Each row must contain 1-4 exactly once
- Each column must contain 1-4 exactly once
- Each 2×2 box must contain 1-4 exactly once

**Skills Measured:** Logical deduction, pattern recognition, constraint satisfaction

---

### 9. Ludo
**Players:** 2-4 | **Difficulty:** Easy | **Time:** 15-45 minutes

**How to Play:**
- Roll dice to move pieces around the board
- Start pieces with a 6, then move clockwise
- Land on opponent pieces to send them back to start
- First player to get all 4 pieces home wins

**Skills Measured:** Risk assessment, probability understanding, decision-making

---

### 10. Reversi (Othello)
**Players:** 2 | **Difficulty:** Medium-Hard | **Time:** 10-30 minutes

**How to Play:**
- Place discs to capture opponent's pieces
- Captured pieces flip to your color
- Must outflank at least one opponent piece with each move
- Player with the most pieces when the board is full wins

**Skills Measured:** Strategic planning, pattern recognition, spatial reasoning

## Performance Tracking

### Performance Index

Your Performance Index is a score from 0-100 that reflects your cognitive performance across games. It considers:

- **Accuracy:** How often you make optimal or winning moves
- **Speed:** Your average time to make decisions
- **Consistency:** How stable your performance is across sessions
- **Improvement:** Whether you're getting better over time

### Skill Tiers

Based on your Performance Index, you're assigned a skill tier:

| Tier | Score Range | Description |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| 🟤 Novice | 0-20 | Just getting started |
| 🟢 Beginner | 21-40 | Learning the basics |
| 🔵 Intermediate | 41-60 | Developing good skills |
| 🟣 Advanced | 61-80 | Strong player |
| 🟠 Expert | 81-100 | Exceptional performance |

### Important Note

⚠️ **This is NOT a clinical IQ test.** The Performance Index is an entertainment-focused metric designed to track your gameplay performance. It should not be used for any diagnostic, academic, or professional purposes.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Play regularly** - Consistency helps track improvement
2. **Try all games** - Different games measure different skills
3. **Take your time** - Rushed decisions may not reflect your true ability
4. **Stay focused** - Minimize distractions during gameplay
5. **Have fun!** - Enjoyment leads to better engagement and performance
